ABOUT

Social service does not require affluence, power or authority all it needs is love, compassion and time. Numerous NGOs have strived tirelessly to make this world a better place and have worked selflessly in the direction of providing a better life to victims of atrocities and injustice. To contribute for a noble cause Sri Vaishnavi Rural Development Society was established in the year 2010-11, with an ideal of eradicating vices of our society such as substance abuse, substance addiction and alcoholism.

Kasturbai B Murradi is the founder of Vaishnavi Rural Development Society. She is an experienced social worker and has worked on the field gaining invaluable exposure in handling issues in Sindagi, Bijapur district in Karnataka. Sri Vaishnavi Rural Development Society has made strides since its establishment in helping individuals by rehabilitating them and ensuring that they walk away from a life rigged with chronic addiction and substance abuse, towards a life of satisfaction and fulfilment.



MISSION

Treating Addictions

Creating an environment that is conducive for habitual change and personal development. Coming into this space that we have created one can rid themselves of their compulsive lifestyle and attain stability and control over their lives.

Journey within

Helping an individual to make a journey inside their minds to help them assess their weaknesses and strengths and thus beginning the process of healing the individual.

A New Life

Preparing an individual to start a new life free of all addictions and compulsions from within and also to a life full of challenges ahead.

Sustainability

Sustenance of this positive change developed here throughout one's life-time even under the force of overwhelming temptation is an intricate part of rehabilitation.

Care-Surveillance

To ensure continuous monitoring in order to impart seamless care and treatment, we have installed CCTV cameras across various places in our Vaishnavi Rural Development Society. This feature also enables us to monitor and check any or all kinds of mischievous / suspicious activities.
